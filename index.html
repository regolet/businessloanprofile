Short Term Loan

Quick access to capital typically repaid within 1-2 years

  • Working capital needs
  • Inventory purchases
  • Equipment & marketing

Most Popular

Long Term Loan

5-10 year terms (potentially 20+) for substantial investments

  • Fixed or floating rates
  • Major expansions
  • Real estate purchases
  • May require collateral

Line of Credit

Revolving access to capital - use what you need, when you need it

  • Pay interest only on used amounts
  • Flexible draw schedule
  • Ideal for cash flow gaps
  • Reusable credit line

Merchant Cash Advance

Non-loan alternative based on future receivables

  • No set term or stated interest
  • Repayment from daily sales
  • Quick funding decision
  • No fixed monthly payments
