### 3. Loan Types - Refined ‚úÖ
**Changed from 6 generic types to 4 specific types matching their site:**

1. **Short Term Loan**
   - Quick access to capital typically repaid within 1-2 years
   - Working capital, inventory, equipment & marketing

2. **Long Term Loan** (Most Popular)
   - 5-10 year terms (potentially 20+)
   - Fixed or floating rates
   - Major expansions, real estate
   - May require collateral

3. **Line of Credit**
   - Revolving access to capital
   - Pay interest only on used amounts
   - Ideal for cash flow gaps
   - Reusable credit line

4. **Merchant Cash Advance**
   - Non-loan alternative based on future receivables
   - No set term or stated interest
   - Repayment from daily sales
   - No fixed monthly payments
